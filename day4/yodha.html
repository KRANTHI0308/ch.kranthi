YODHA

Yodha (Warrior) is a 2024 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Sagar Ambre and Pushkar Ojha. Ambre also wrote the screenplay, while it was produced by Hiroo Yash Johar, Karan Johar and Apoorva Mehta under the banner of Dharma Productions.The film stars Sidharth Malhotra, Raashii Khanna and Disha Patani. Yodha was theatrically released on 15 March 2024.It underperformed at the box office.
