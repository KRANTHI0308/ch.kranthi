KHILADI 786

Khiladi 786 is a 2012 Indian Bollywood action comedy film directed by Ashish R. Mohan .The film stars Akshay Kumar and Asin in the lead roles, with Mithun Chakraborty , Manoj Joshi and Himesh Reshammiya in important supporting roles. It is produced by Eros International , H. Music Ltd. and Hari Om Entertainment . With this film, Akshay Kumar returned to the Khiladi series after 12 years.

The film's first trailer was released online on 4 October. It received 1.5 million hits in 5 days and some industry experts said that it would be the biggest opening day grosser of Akshay's film career.The full trailer was released on 12 November and the film released on 7 December 2012.
